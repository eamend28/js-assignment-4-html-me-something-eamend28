My first Night

>

I had just moved away from everyone I know to take a sales job in DC. I had no money and no friends. When I got to my apartment I also had no furniture. So that first night I dumped my suitcase out on the floor and made a pile out of clothes. I slept on that pile and used my coat as a blanket.

First day of Work

The first day of work my boss came picked me up becasue I did not have a car yet. She drove me all around the city and took me to eat. I did not know this lady at all, but she seemed nice enough!

Found the Basketball Court

When I got home that day I had a lot of nervous energy I needed to get rid of. I went to the park nearby and found a basketball court. There were some pretty good players there so I joined the game. Come to find out they play there everyday after work. The court is where I made my only friends in DC!
